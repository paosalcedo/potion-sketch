poetry flight

“Poetry Flight” is a game about deconstructing and reconstructing text. Paste text such as actual poetry, obnoxious tweets, and annoying news articles, then experience it in a whole new flight.

“Voyeur at the Edge of the Universe” was my final project for Worlds On A Wire–an NYU ITP class taught by Todd Bryant. I wanted to prototype a way to experience stories in VR without moving the camera independent of the player–a common cause of VR-induced dizziness or nausea. “Voyeur” contains three different scenes or rooms, each with a different story; the player can control when or if each story progresses–kind of the way a DJ would mix and match different musical tracks in a live performance. In the future, it would be interesting to add more story controls such as rewinding and fast-forwarding, as well as being able to transfer characters from one scene to another to change the story. Made with MagicaVoxel, Mixamo, and Unreal Engine 4.

CoMix is an AR comics experiment that lets you change the story when you rearrange the panels. I wanted to create a speculative AR project that would let comic book readers enjoy the benefits of AR without sacrificing the joy and tactility of holding the printed page–or panels, in this case. Many thanks to Nico Zapanta for the “Action Star” art. Made in Unity with Vuforia.
